Abiotics


Abiotics are non-living things that play a role in an ecosystem/ecosystems.

Water, the Sun, wind, etc are examples of Abiotics!




Biotics


Biotics are living things!

Living things are things that can breathe, grow, etc

These Livings things may have ecosystems in themselves!

Biotics also play a role in one or many ecosystems!

Animals, plants, bacteria and organisms, etc are examples of biotics!


Fun Fact

Humans have ecosystems in themselves! Since there is 8 billion humans there are 8 billion different ecosystems!
